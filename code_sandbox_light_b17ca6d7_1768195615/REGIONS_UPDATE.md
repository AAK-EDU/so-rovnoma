## üîç Common Questions

**Q: Why is Tashkent listed twice?**
A: Tashkent City (the capital) and Tashkent Region are separate administrative divisions. The city has special status, while the region surrounds it.

**Q: Why "Qashqadaryo" instead of "Kashkadarya"?**
A: "Qashqadaryo" is the official Latin spelling according to Uzbekistan's naming conventions. We use official spellings for accuracy.

**Q: Is Karakalpakstan different from other regions?**
A: Yes, it's an autonomous republic within Uzbekistan with special constitutional status, hence listed second after the capital.

**Q: Why show all three languages?**
A: This ensures all users can identify their region regardless of which language they're most comfortable with, improving data accuracy.
